The objective of this lab is to familiarize yourself with solvers, classification of optimization problems, and what algorithm to choose. You can and are expected to use online resources. Most of your time is likely to be spent learning about your selected optimization platform.

You are free to use any optimization platform you prefer. Suggested ones are: R, Python, MATLAB/YALMIP, Julia. Ask for TA for more information on any of those; and definitely ask permission from your TA if you wish to use another platform (so your work can be graded).
