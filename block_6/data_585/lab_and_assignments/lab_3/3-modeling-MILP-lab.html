You are free to use any optimization platform you prefer. Suggested ones are: R, Python, MATLAB/YALMIP, Julia. Ask for TA for more information on any of those; and definitely ask permission from your TA if you wish to use another platform (so your work can be graded).

  1. Solve using software the following LP $\max_{x_1,x_2} 5x_1+3 x_2$ subject to $3x_1+5x_2\leq 20$, $2x_1+x_2\leq 7.5$, $x_1-x_2\leq 2$, $x_1\geq 0$, and $x_2\geq 0$.
  2. Solve the same problem with the additional constraints $x_1\in \Z$.
  3. We saw in class the 4 types of possibilities for LP problems. Implement the examples for Possibilities 2,3,4 to see how your solver deals with it. Note: You might encounter weird outcomes. This question will make you aware of such outcomes.
    Possibility 2:
    $\max_x x$ subject to $x=0$, $x\geq 0$.
    Possibility 3:
    $\max_x x$ subject to $x=-1$, $x\geq 0$ whose dual is $\min_y -y$ subject to $y\geq 1$.
    Possibility 4:
    $\max_{x_1, x_2, x_3} x_1$ subject to $x_1-x_2=1$, $x_3=-1$, and $x_1, x_2, x_3 \geq 0$.
